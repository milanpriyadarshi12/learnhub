Pricing FAQs

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll only pay the difference for the remaining period. Downgrades take effect from the next billing cycle.

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and Wallets. EMI options are also available on select banks.

Yes! Students with a valid college ID can get an additional 20% discount on any plan. Contact our support team with your student ID to avail this offer.
Can I share my account?
No, LearnHub accounts are for individual use only. Sharing accounts violates our terms of service and may result in account suspension. We offer family plans at discounted rates if multiple people in your household want to learn.
